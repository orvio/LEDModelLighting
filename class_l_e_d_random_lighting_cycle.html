Detailed Description

This class toggles between on and off state based on the minimum and maximum duration for each state.

The times required for the _offToOnEffect and _onToOffEffect eat into the the on time and off time respectively. The transition effects will always play out in full, even when they take longer than the total time alotted for the on or off state they are assigned to.
